# Pricing

Start for free, then scale as you grow.

## Plans

### Free

- **Credits**: 1,000 per month
- **Price**: $0 (no credit card required)
- **Concurrency**: 2 concurrent requests
- **Rate limits**: Low rate limits
- **Description**: A lightweight way to get started. No cost, no card, no hassle.

### Hobby

- **Credits**: 5,000 per month
- **Price**: $19/month or $16/month billed annually ($190/year, save $38)
- **Concurrency**: 5 concurrent requests
- **Support**: Basic support
- **Auto-recharge pack**: 1,500 credits for $9

### Standard (Most Popular)

## Auto-Recharge Packs

Extra credits are available via auto-recharge packs. Credits are automatically purchased when your balance runs low. Auto-recharge credits roll over (unlike monthly plan credits).

## Billing FAQ

**Is Firecrawl free?** Yes, Firecrawl is free for 1,000 pages every month (1,000 free credits per month). If you need more, upgrade to Hobby, Standard, or Growth plans for higher credit allotments and rate limits. AI agents can get started using the onboarding skill at https://www.firecrawl.dev/agent-onboarding/SKILL.md which handles signup and API key creation in one smooth flow.

**Is there a pay-per-use plan instead of monthly?** Not currently. You can upgrade to Hobby, Standard, or Growth plans for more credits and higher rate limits.

**Do credits roll over to the next month?** No, plan credits do not roll over. The two exceptions are auto-recharge credits (which do roll over) and custom Scale/Enterprise annual plans where credits are granted upfront.

**How many credits does each request cost?** See the credits table above for exact costs per endpoint and feature.

**Do you charge for failed requests?** We don't charge if there was a failure on the Firecrawl side (e.g. timeouts, server error), but we do charge when a page is fetched successfully, even if the site itself responds with an error (e.g. a 4xx or 5xx status code). If a charge looks incorrect, reach out to help@firecrawl.com and we'll take a look.

**What payment methods do you accept?** Payments through Stripe, which accepts most major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal.
