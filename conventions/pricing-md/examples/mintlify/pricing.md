## Credits & AI billing

- AI features (assistant, agent, workflows) are billed using credits.
- **Starter:** 5,000 credits to trial. Buy additional credit packages to use beyond the trial.
- **Enterprise:** 5,000 credits included. $0.01 per credit for overages. Tiered packages available at discounted rates.
- You can set a hard spending limit or turn off overages entirely from your dashboard.
- Each plan includes a set amount of credits per month; overages are billed at $0.01/credit.

## FAQ

**How can we migrate our existing content?**
You can use our [public packages](https://www.mintlify.com/docs/guides/migration) to convert your existing documentation to Mintlify. We currently support automated migration for Readme and Docusaurus. If you don't see your docs provider or have a home-grown system, get in touch at gtm@mintlify.com and we can help.

**Are LLMs used to power the assistant?**
We use third-party model providers to power our AI features. To respond to a query, we perform semantic and full-text searches across your documentation to build context for the model. None of the providers we use will train on your data.

**How does pricing work for AI features such as the assistant, agent, and workflows?**
AI features are billed using credits. Each plan includes a set amount of credits per month and overages are billed at $0.01/credit. You can set a hard spending limit or turn off overages entirely from your dashboard. Tiered credit packages are also available at discounted rates if you need more.

**How long is my free trial?**
After signing up for Mintlify, you get 14 days to try the product for free — no credit card required. All AI features are available during your trial with up to 5,000 credits included.

**Can I set a hard limit on AI credits?**
Yes. The dashboard includes controls that let you either turn off overages and pause AI features when you reach your plan quota, or stay enrolled in overages and continue using them with spending controls. If your limit is reached and you have disabled overages, AI features automatically turn off until your next billing cycle. You can also set up email notifications when approaching your spending thresholds.

**What auth methods are supported?**
We offer multiple forms of authentication: password protection, JWT, OAuth, or Mintlify Auth (our internal user authentication solution). You can make specific pages viewable without authentication at the page or group level with the [public property](https://www.mintlify.com/docs/deploy/authentication-setup#make-pages-public).

**Can I use the AI features on the Starter plan?**
Yes, AI features are available on the Starter plan. You get up to 5,000 credits included at no cost. If you need more, additional credits can be purchased directly from the dashboard on your existing plan.

**Are you SOC 2 certified and what other security measures are in place?**
Yes, we are SOC 2 Type 2 certified and GDPR compliant. We are currently in the process of becoming ISO 27001 compliant. You can find the full report alongside other security policies on our [trust center](https://security.mintlify.com).
