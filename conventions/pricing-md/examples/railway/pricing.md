# Pricing

Railway charges per-second for the CPU, memory, and disk your services actually use. No overprovisioning, no idle markup, no surprises. Outgoing bandwidth and object storage are billed per GB.

## Plans

- Free Trial — One-time $5 credit grant to try Railway. No credit card required.
- Hobby — $5/month with included usage. For solo developers and side projects.
- Pro — $20/month per seat. For teams shipping to production. Higher limits, longer log retention, and team features.
- Enterprise — Custom pricing. Contractual SLAs, SSO, dedicated support, and procurement options. See https://railway.com/enterprise.

## Usage-based prices

Resource consumption is billed in addition to the plan fee, per second:

- Memory — per GB / second
- CPU — per vCPU / second
- Volumes — per GB / second
- Egress — per GB (services)
- Object Storage — per GB-month, free egress

Exact rates are shown on the pricing page and in the @railway/pricing package; they're the same numbers used in your billing.

## Committed spend

Annual committed spend discounts are available for teams on Pro and Enterprise. Talk to sales for details.
